About the Incubation Centre

The Incubation Centre at Government Science College, Jabalpur aims to support aspiring innovators, student entrepreneurs, and early-stage startups through mentorship, resources, and research backing. We envision fostering a culture of innovation and sustainable entrepreneurship across multiple domains.

Message from the Director

Prof. Ankita Bohare
Director, Incubation Centre

"We are committed to nurturing young minds and enabling research-driven innovations in the heart of Madhya Pradesh. The Incubation Centre provides a vibrant space for collaboration between academia, industry, and young entrepreneurs."

Focus Areas

Cybersecurity & Blockchain

Supporting research and product development in secure communication, threat modeling, and distributed systems.

AgriTech & Sustainability

Encouraging ideas in smart farming, hydroponics, and eco-tech that solve real-world rural challenges.

AI & Data Science

Developing tools and startups working in artificial intelligence, computer vision, and big data analytics.

Social Innovation

Solutions for education, health, and public welfare using low-cost technology and community engagement.
